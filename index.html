Nepal's Pride

Rhododendron

Rhododendron is the national flower of Nepal.Rhododendrons and azaleas perform best in regions with cool, moist summers. They bloom in late spring to early summer; flower colors include pink, red, violet, yellow, and white, depending on the species and variety. Size also varies considerably, from 1 or 2 feet to over 20 feet in height, so choose plants carefully to fit your garden situation. Although the distinction isn't clearcut, in general rhododendrons are evergreen, while most azaleas are deciduous (although there are evergreen types).

Lophophorus

Danfe (Lophophorus) is national bird of Nepal.This species of bird has common name as Pheasant, Monal, Danfe are commonly found in certain parts of Nepal, India, Tibet but eliminated from Afghanistan.Both male and female have different look.Males are more colorful than females.Though these gentle birds are also tamed but found wildly in the mountain ranges.It is in the list of most beautiful bird in the kingdom of Nepal.Not, only it’s on the most beautiful bird’s list, it is also in the list of endangered species in Nepal.

cow

Cow is the national animal of Nepal. Most of the people of Nepal are Hindus and cow is worshipped as the goddess of wealth.Killing of cow is prohibited in the hindu religion and in Nepal.The cow has been a part of our civilisation since forever,revered by some and just treated as a road-blocking nuisance by others. But it is this humble animal that has gone on to become the national animal of Nepal.There were a tough competition between the cow and the one-horned rhino but then,looks like simplicity won over extravagance.
